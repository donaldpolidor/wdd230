About the Community

The Haitian Chamber of Commerce is an organization dedicated to supporting and promoting local businesses while contributing to the country's economic development. It serves as a platform for commercial exchanges, encourages business partnerships and facilitates networking for Haitian entrepreneurs. Through strategic initiatives, training and events, the Chamber of Commerce works to improve the business environment, stimulate investment and foster the growth of the Haitian economy. It plays a key role in strengthening the competitiveness of local businesses and creating new opportunities for the Haitian community.
