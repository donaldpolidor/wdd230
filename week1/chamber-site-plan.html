Site map : Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce

Website name

Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce

The name reflects the organization's geographic location and purpose: to promote business and economic interests in Port-au-Prince.

Site purpose

  • Promote local businesses and attract investment.
  • Provide information on economic opportunities and local events.

Scenarios

  • What are the advantages of joining the Chamber of Commerce?
  • How can I find local business partners?
  • What events are organized to promote local trade?

Color scheme

  • Green (#4CAF50) : used for headers and footers.
  • Light grey (#f9f9f9) : used for section backgrounds.

Typography

  • Roboto Bold : used for titles.
  • Roboto Regular : used for body text.
